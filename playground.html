What to try

Put many points close together and leave one or two far away. With a radius that covers the cluster, clustered points get high friend counts and outliers get low friend counts. Press the run button several times: points with q(c)=1 always stay, points with q(c)=0 always disappear, and middle points may flip because FriendlyCore is randomized. Tap empty space to add a point, drag points to move them, and double tap a point to remove it.
