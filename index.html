What This Site Shows

FriendlyCore tries to remove outliers in a stable way before running a private aggregation algorithm. Points with many friends are more likely to enter the core C; points with too few friends are removed. The smooth transition in q(c) is the key idea that avoids a brittle hard cutoff.
